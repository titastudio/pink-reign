💖 PINK REIGN
You are Vivienne. It girl. Campus apex predator. Social architect. Your 4-year university run determines whether you graduate as a Pop Idol, Political Princess, Cult Empress, or something far darker.
GAME AT A GLANCE
🗓 Time: 4 years × 12 weeks. 3 actions per week.
📊 Stats: 6 public + 17 hidden. Both matter.
💌 Letters: ? letters across 6 categories.
🛍 Items: ? luxury pieces. Social weapons.
💋 Boys: 8 archetypes + 5 marriage candidates.
👑 Endings: 9 graduation routes to unlock.
🎀 Cult: 5 philosophies × 6 evolution stages.
👭 Factions: 5 campus tribes. Navigate all.
📱 Social: Dynamic PinkFeed + virality system.
🌸 Aesthetics: ? profiles. Each changes your orbit.
👑 Queens: 6 queen bee archetypes to challenge or ally.
🔄 Rivals: 6 evolution paths. They never stay down.
🏛 Dynasty: 5 house types. Shapes every generation.
🌍 Generations: Multi-protagonist timeline. Legacy matters.
ALL GAME SYSTEMS
📋 ACTIONS (Main Window)
3 per week. Mix stat investment vs social maneuvering. Cash actions cost money. Advance week when done.
💌 LETTERS (Mailbox)
Fan, Hate, Boyfriend, Cult, Political, General. Effects apply when opened. Filter by category. Unread = ● marker. Post-marriage letters from spouse appear once married.
👜 BOUTIQUE (Shop)
Luxury items are social weapons. Each has rarity (Common/Rare/Legendary), social lore, and faction opinion shifts. Owned = permanent passive bonuses applied at purchase.
🎀 INNER CIRCLE (Cult)
Choose one of 5 cult philosophies. Recruit followers (need 20+ Influence). Each follower has a loyalty type. More followers = passive influence weekly. Track cult evolution across 6 stages in the Cult Evo window.
💋 BOYS
Date 1 boy at a time. Each applies weekly stat effects. Multi-bf harem possible but risks exposure. Boyfriend type affects graduation routes. Use BF Chat for intimacy progression (5 stages). Loyalty tracked via 7 hidden variables.
💬 BF CHAT & INTIMACY
Send messages with different tones (Flirt, Check-In, Possessive, Cold, Vulnerable). Each BF archetype replies differently. Intimacy progresses through 5 stages with milestone dialogs. Secret mode archives chats separately but raises exposure risk. Post-intimacy states (Obsessed, Possessive, Dependent, Withdrawn, Romantic) apply weekly effects.
📱 PINKFEED (Social)
Campus social media. Reflects your current stats and events. Refresh to see new posts. "Go Viral" triggers a major social event. Feed updates dynamically with your game state. Virality window lets you post specific content types.
👭 FACTIONS
5 campus tribes each track your rep based on your stats and actions. Clean Girls, Coquettes, Academic Elites, Rebels, Luxury Wives. High rep = exclusive opportunities. Low rep = rumors and obstacles. Aesthetic profile affects faction relations.
🌸 AESTHETICS (Stats Window)
Your aesthetic profile shapes which followers are attracted, which factions favor you, and which events become likely. Switch anytime — it shifts your orbit and faction relationships immediately.
⚠ CAMPUS EVENTS
Trigger every 4 weeks automatically. Branching choices with stat requirements. Each outcome modifies your hidden stats. Some events are permanent. Personality archetypes unlock additional event choices.
📡 VIRALITY (Virality Window)
Post content with Visibility, Controversy, and Timing scores. Success type (Admired/Hated/Meme/Mysterious) is weighted by your stats. Viral posts appear in PinkFeed. Can trigger campus events. Builds viral momentum over time.
🕯 OBSESSION TRACKER (Obsessed Window)
Fame and beauty attract obsessors. 7 NPC types: fan, stalker, cult, admirer, ex, rival, confidant. Each escalates weekly. High-level obsessors send threatening letters. Manage: Ignore (escalates), Encourage (dangerous), Weaponize (vs rivals, costs purity), Block.
🧠 PERSONALITY DRIFT (Psyche Window)
5 personality axes drift weekly based on your choices: Empathy, Narcissism, Paranoia, Ambition, Emotional Stability. Crossing thresholds unlocks Personality Archetypes (The Hollow Crown, God Complex, etc.) with permanent passive effects that modify available event choices.
💍 ARRANGED MARRIAGE (Marriage Window)
Family pressure rises each year. 5 candidates presented at 30+ pressure: Old Money, Politician, Tycoon, Foreign Prince, Rebel Heir. Negotiate 4 term categories: freedom, inheritance, appearances, affairs clause (needs 50 Intel). Accept, reject, or manipulate. Unlocks Trophy Wife and First Lady routes.
💔 RELATIONSHIP WARFARE (Rel. War Window)
BF Loyalty system tracks 7 hidden variables (Devotion, Jealousy, Dependency, Ego, Temptation Resistance, Fear, Ambition). Rivals launch temptation attacks on your boyfriend. Exes persist as world entities. 5 evolving relationship dynamic states.
👑 QUEEN BEE HIERARCHY (Queens Window)
6 Queen Bee archetypes rule different campus spheres. Each has a strategy, threat level, and weekly actions against you. Challenge them via Reputation Combat when your dominance score is high enough. Ally with them for network bonuses. Defeated queens evolve — they don't disappear.
⚔ REPUTATION WARFARE (Rep Battle Window)
Social combat system. No fists — only perception. Choose from 6 attack moves (Whisper Campaign, Scandal Drop, Beauty Armor, Cult Support, Influence Surge, Emotional Control). Build combos by varying moves. 8 turns to win before the crowd loses interest. Stat-scaled damage.
💘 COUPLE BRAND & HAREM (Brand Window)
5 couple brand archetypes (Campus Royalty, Dangerous Power, Tragic Romance, Toxic Obsession, Secret Harem Queen). Each unlocks based on your stats and relationship state. Brands apply weekly passive effects. Multi-BF harem is possible but raises exposure risk +15%/lover per week.
🔄 RIVAL EVOLUTION (Rival Evo Window)
Defeated rivals don't stay down. 6 evolution paths: Redemption Arc, Revenge Awakening, Cult Integration, Full Reinvention, Political Comeback, Broken State. Each path gives the rival ongoing weekly passive actions against you. Track all evolutions in the Rival Evo window.
🏛 DYNASTY & LEGACY (Dynasty House + Legacy Windows)
Choose your House type (Political, Entertainment, Luxury, Cult Bloodline, Academic Elite). Each gives weekly buffs and burdens. Dynasty carries across generations, giving starting bonuses to your next protagonist. Social Era shifts each generation (Old Money → Influencer → Digital Politics → Virtual Cult → Post-Dynasty).
🕯 CULT EVOLUTION (Cult Evo Window)
Your cult grows across 6 stages: Dormant → Secret Circle → Campus Influence → Political Movement → Religious Institution → Cultural Legacy. Progress is based on followers, cult suspicion, and inherited generations. Higher stages unlock faction mutations and cross-generation power.
🩷 HEIR COMPATIBILITY (Heir Window)
Profile your potential heir with weighted trait generation (Rebellious, Genius, Manipulative, Fragile, Power Hungry, etc.). Compatibility score based on dynasty type, marriage status, and cult stage. Low compatibility = succession war. High = smooth next-gen start.
🪞 TIMELINE (Timeline Window)
Multi-protagonist timeline shows every past generation's protagonist and their stats, route, aesthetic, and legacy choice. Click any entry to see her full dossier. Current generation appears at the bottom. Lore entries from all generations accumulate.
🔥 WORLD STATE (World State Window)
Permanent world-level consequences from your actions persist across generations. World modifiers apply ongoing effects to your stats. Scandals, cult influence, political actions, and luxury investments all leave world-state marks that the next protagonist inherits.
⚔ SUCCESSION WARS
Triggered at generation transitions when heir compatibility is low or world modifiers are destabilizing. Branching crisis events with major stat consequences. Winning preserves dynasty continuity; losing reshapes the world state and strips starting bonuses.
💾 SAVE SYSTEM (Start Menu)
Save locally to browser. Export JSON file to disk. Import from file. Auto-saves on most actions. No cloud required. Save includes world history, all window positions, and full game state.
📸 PAPARAZZI (Paparazzi Window)
Fame and scandal generate paparazzi heat. High heat = photo capture events. Each photo has 4 response options (Deny, Embrace, Spin, Sue). High media aggression locks some routes. Polaroid-style photos accumulate in your archive.
🩷 DOUBLE LIFE (Double Life Window)
Public and hidden personas diverge as you make dark choices. Contrast score rises as the gap grows. Exposure meter tracks leak risk. Actions let you manage both sides of your identity. When exposure hits 100, the reveal event triggers.
🎭 SOCIAL MASKS (Masks Window)
Equip different masks for different social groups. Each mask costs identity stability. Too many active masks = mask integrity drop. When mask integrity fails, the authentic self breaks through — sometimes beneficially, sometimes catastrophically.
🌙 NIGHT EVENTS (Night Window)
High-reward, high-risk events that unfold after dark. Exclusive parties, underground meetings, secret rendezvous. Night mode gives ×1.3 reward multiplier. Actions used here don't consume your weekly action limit but have unique risk profiles.
💄 BEAUTY CLINIC (Clinic Window)
7 beauty procedures from subtle (lip filler) to extreme (full rhinoplasty). Each has Beauty stat gains, dependency risk, and instability cost. High dependency = weekly withdrawal penalty. High instability = random beauty crashes. Addiction spiral is real.
🎰 FATE & DESTINY (Fate Window)
Weekly fate rolls introduce random events outside your control. Event probability is biased by your lifestyle and stats (high scandal = bad luck rolls more often). Fate log tracks every random event. Some fate events are permanent.
🎀 PERSONAL STAFF (Staff Window)
Hire 5 specialist staff: Stylist, Publicist, Fixer, Bodyguard, Informant. Each costs weekly cash and provides passive bonuses. Staff have loyalty scores — mistreat them and they betray you. Bodyguard improves territory defense calculations.
🧸 TROPHY ROOM (Trophies Window)
Auto-collects legendary achievements: landmark purchases, milestone stats, major event victories, combat wins. Trophies are permanent records. They carry over to the next generation as inherited prestige. Full trophy room unlocks the Legacy route.
🏫 CAMPUS TERRITORY (Territory Window)
6 campus zones claimable with faction rep. Each owned zone gives weekly passive bonuses. High-threat Queen Bees invade owned zones at 15% chance per week. Your defense score is based on influence, popularity, staff (bodyguard), and faction relations with the zone's affiliated faction.
📖 LORE ARCHIVE (Lore Window)
Every major event, battle, alliance, scandal, and milestone gets auto-logged with week/year context. Lore entries accumulate across generations. The last entry from each past generation appears in the Timeline window as their epitaph.
🏛 SECRET SOCIETIES (Societies Window)
4 elite societies with stat-gated entry requirements. Joining costs something meaningful (a sacrifice). Active membership gives weekly bonuses unavailable elsewhere. Societies interact with graduation routes — some are required for specific endings.
💔 TRAUMA SYSTEM (Trauma Window)
5 trauma types accumulated through extreme events. Each trauma modifies available choices in future events. 4 coping styles: Suppress (short-term gain, long-term cost), Process (slow but healthy), Channel (weaponize it), Deny (unstable but powerful). Active traumas are displayed as tags.
💡 STRATEGY GUIDE
EARLY GAME (Yr 1, Wks 1–12)
Decide your route by Week 8. Idol needs Beauty+Popularity. CEO needs Elegance+Intelligence. Cult Empress needs Influence+CultSuspicion. Don't spread thin — pick 2-3 stats and go deep. Founding your cult early gives compound follower bonuses all four years.

BOYFRIEND STRATEGY
The Mafia Heir stacks scandal fast — good for Rebel and Dangerous Power routes, dangerous for Trophy Wife. The Nerd quietly maxes intelligence. The Idol creates viral exposure explosions — use in Year 3. Dark Artist + high instability unlocks specific campus events. Dump and re-date strategically: each BF type shapes your personality drift differently.

INTIMACY SYSTEM
Reach Intimacy Stage 3 (Attached) with your primary BF for the best weekly loyalty bonuses. Secret mode protects purity but adds +5 exposure per message. Post-intimacy state "Obsessed" gives the most volatile but powerful dynamic — high scandal risk, high influence reward.

HIDDEN STATS
Watch Instability. Above 50 = Mysterious Disappearance ending locks in. Purity below 30 enables Rebel route. CultSuspicion above 30 is needed for Cult Empress. Empathy above 70 unlocks "Empath Weapon" archetype — converts rivals to allies. Narcissism above 60 + followers = God Complex archetype (uncontrollable cult growth).

QUEEN BEE STRATEGY
Don't challenge queens until your Dominance score meets their threshold. Ally first with lower-threat queens to build your network, then use that network strength in rep combat vs high-threat queens. The Chaos Queen is the hardest to beat — her attack value is 11. Lead with Cult Support and Influence Surge.

REPUTATION COMBAT
Never use the same attack twice in a row — combo breaks to ×1. Vary moves to stack combo up to ×4. Your stat loadout dictates best moves: high beauty → Beauty Armor, many followers → Cult Support, high influence → Influence Surge. Win before turn 6 for maximum prestige.

COUPLE BRAND TIPS
Campus Royalty brand gives the safest weekly bonus. Dangerous Power needs Mafia Heir or 25+ scandal. Secret Harem triggers at 2+ lovers but adds +15% exposure risk per lover per week — keep it brief or hire the Fixer to suppress leaks. Declare Exclusive to reset exposure risk if harem gets dangerous.

RIVAL EVOLUTION
Defeating a rival triggers evolution 3 seconds later. Broken State is the safe fallback but Redemption Arc rivals are uniquely dangerous — they become loveable, eating your sympathy base. Revenge rivals escalate threat to 65. If you see a Revenge evolution, prioritize starting a new Rep Combat before she acts.

FACTION TIPS
Academic Elites boost intelligence events. Clean Girls open old-money and marriage opportunities. Rebels unlock underground paths and the Rebel route. Luxury Wives are needed for Trophy Wife route. Don't ignore factions — they spread campus-wide rumors when rep is low. Aesthetic switches directly affect faction relations.

LETTER TIMING
BF Letters only appear when you're dating that archetype. Political letters spike in Year 3. Cult letters increase as followers grow. Obsession letters escalate weekly — manage your Obsession NPC tracker or threatening letters will spike your scandal. Post-marriage letters from spouses provide ongoing stat effects.

SOCIAL MEDIA & VIRALITY
High visibility + high controversy = viral. Type matters: beauty/elegance players go admired-viral, scandal players go hate-viral. Mysterious viral needs cult suspicion + obsession. Time posts in Year 2–4 for max impact — viral momentum carries between posts. Viral events can trigger campus event chains worth doing late game.

OBSESSION MANAGEMENT
Obsessors appear silently. Check the Obsession window weekly. Admirers send cash at low obsession levels — let them linger safely. Stalkers and exes become dangerous past 70 obsession. Weaponizing obsessors against rivals costs purity but eliminates threats instantly. Block if you need to — exposure is worse than losing the cash.

PERSONALITY DRIFT
Emotional Stability below 25 triggers random scandal outbursts weekly — guard it. Paranoia above 60 closes off alliance-based event choices. Ambition above 70 boosts all influence actions. The Hollow Crown archetype (high narcissism + low empathy) is powerful but permanently removes "vulnerable" event choices.

MARRIAGE ROUTE
For Trophy Wife: max beauty+elegance+wealth, then marry Edward or Marco. For First Lady: marry Sebastian (politician) or any candidate while dating William/Henri. Negotiate affairs clause (needs 50 Intel) if you plan to keep boyfriends post-marriage. Nico Beaumont gives maximum freedom but enrages your family — Family Control drops 10.

TERRITORY CONTROL
Owning zones gives weekly passive bonuses. Hire the Bodyguard (Staff window) — it adds +20 to your territory defense calculation. Match your zone to your faction rep: if your Academic Elite rep is high, claim the Library first. Queens with threat >40 attempt invasion at 15% chance per week — counter by raising your dominance score.

CASH FLOW
Allowance scales with Wealth stat. High wealth = more money each week. Luxury Dynasty gives +$300/week. Buy Private Jet last — it's the biggest wealth + influence bomb in the game and instantly shifts you to international status. Hire staff cautiously — their weekly salary compounds.

DYNASTY STRATEGY
Set your House type in Year 2 at the latest. The stat bonus applies immediately. Political dynasty gives ongoing influence; Luxury gives $300/week; Entertainment boosts popularity. Your chosen type persists to the next generation — pick the one that matches your graduation route, not just your current stats.

CULT EVOLUTION
Cult stage scales with followers + cult suspicion + generations inherited. Reach Campus Influence (Stage 2) to unlock faction mutations. Stage 3+ makes your cult a political force across generations. Cult Bloodline dynasty gives 2 starting followers and faster suspicion scaling — ideal for Cult Empress route.

HEIR COMPATIBILITY
Set dynasty type + be married + have high cult stage for maximum compatibility score. The "Power Hungry" heir trait (+25 compat) is the best outcome — push ambition above 60 and influence above 60 to weight it. Low compatibility (<50) risks a Succession War event that strips starting bonuses.

BEAUTY CLINIC WARNING
Every procedure raises dependency. Above 50 dependency = weekly withdrawal penalty when you don't purchase. Above 30 instability = random beauty crashes. Use the Natural Beauty Routine action to slowly reduce both stats. Addiction spiral is intentional — the Hollow route requires high dependency as a thematic mirror.
