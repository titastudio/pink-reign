💖 PINK REIGN
You are Vivienne. It girl. Campus apex predator. Social architect. Your 4-year university run determines whether you graduate as a Pop Idol, Political Princess, Cult Empress, or something far darker.
GAME AT A GLANCE
🗓 Time: 4 years × 4 weeks. 2 actions per week.
📊 Stats: 6 public + 17 hidden. Both matter.
💌 Letters: ? letters across 6 categories.
🛍 Items: ? luxury pieces. Social weapons.
💋 Boys: 8 archetypes. Each week reshapes you.
👑 Endings: 9 graduation routes to unlock.
🎀 Cult: 5 philosophies. Build your empire.
👭 Factions: 5 campus tribes. Navigate all.
📱 Social: Dynamic feed. Campus gossip lives.
🌸 Aesthetics: ? profiles. Each changes your orbit.
ALL GAME SYSTEMS
📋 ACTIONS (Main Window)
2 per week. Mix stat investment vs social maneuvering. Cash actions cost money. Advance week when done.
💌 LETTERS (Mailbox)
Fan, Hate, Boyfriend, Cult, Political, General. Each letter applies stat effects when opened. Filter by category. Unread = ● marker.
👜 BOUTIQUE (Shop)
Luxury items are social weapons. Each has rarity, social meaning, and faction opinion. Owned = permanent passive bonuses applied at purchase.
🎀 INNER CIRCLE (Cult)
Choose one of 5 cult philosophies. Recruit followers (need 20+ Influence). Each follower has a loyalty type. More followers = passive influence weekly.
💋 BOYS
Date 1 boy at a time. Each applies weekly stat effects. Breaking up ends the bonus. Boyfriend type affects certain graduation routes.
📱 PINKFEED (Social)
Campus social media. Reflects your current stats. Refresh to see new posts. "Go Viral" triggers a major social event. Feed updates dynamically with your game state.
👭 FACTIONS
5 campus tribes each track your rep with them based on your stats and actions. High rep = exclusive opportunities. Low rep = rumors and obstacles.
🌸 AESTHETICS (Stats Window)
Your aesthetic profile shapes which followers are attracted, which factions favor you, and which events become likely. Switch anytime — it shifts your orbit.
⚠ CAMPUS EVENTS
Trigger every 3 weeks automatically. Branching choices. Each outcome modifies your hidden stats. Some events are permanent.
💾 SAVE SYSTEM (Start Menu)
Save locally. Export JSON file to disk. Import from file. Auto-saves optional via Start Menu. No cloud required.
📡 VIRALITY (Virality Window)
Post content with visibility, controversy, and timing sliders. Success type (Admired/Hated/Meme/Mysterious) is weighted by your stats. Viral posts appear in PinkFeed. Can trigger campus events. Builds viral momentum over time.
🕯 OBSESSION TRACKER (Obsessed Window)
Fame and beauty attract obsessors. Each NPC escalates weekly. Types: fan, stalker, cult, admirer, ex, rival. High-level obsessors send threatening fan mail and take dangerous actions. Manage them: ignore (escalates), encourage (dangerous), weaponize (against rivals), or block.
🧠 PERSONALITY DRIFT (Psyche Window)
5 personality axes drift weekly based on your choices: Empathy, Narcissism, Paranoia, Ambition, Emotional Stability. When thresholds are crossed, you unlock a Personality Archetype (e.g. The Hollow Crown, The God Complex) with permanent passive effects. Archetypes modify available event choices.
💍 ARRANGED MARRIAGE (Marriage Window)
Family pressure rises each year. 5 candidates presented at 30+ pressure. Negotiate freedom, inheritance, appearances, and affairs clause (needs 50 intelligence). Accept, reject, or manipulate terms. Marriage unlocks enhanced Trophy Wife and First Lady routes plus post-marriage letters from your spouse.
💡 STRATEGY GUIDE
EARLY GAME (Yr 1-2)
Decide your route early. Idol needs Beauty+Popularity. CEO needs Elegance+Intelligence. Pick 2-3 stats and go deep. Don't spread thin.

BOYFRIEND STRATEGY
The Mafia Heir stacks scandal fast — good for Rebel route, dangerous for Trophy Wife. The Nerd quietly maxes intelligence. Idol dating creates scandal explosions. Use carefully.

HIDDEN STATS
Watch Instability. Above 50 = Mysterious Disappearance ending locks in. Purity below 30 enables Rebel route. CultSuspicion above 30 is needed for Cult Empress.

FACTION TIPS
Academic Elites boost intelligence events. Clean Girls open old-money opportunities. Rebels unlock underground paths. Don't ignore factions — they spread rumors.

LETTER TIMING
BF Letters only appear when you're dating that archetype. Political letters spike in Year 3. Cult letters increase as followers grow. Read ALL letters — effects apply immediately.

SOCIAL MEDIA
Refresh PinkFeed after major events. "Go Viral" is high-risk high-reward — popularity spike but scandal risk. Use sparingly in Year 3-4.

AESTHETICS
Switching aesthetic is free. "Sad Rich Girl" attracts cult followers. "Royal Academia" boosts Academic Elite faction rep. "Feminine Dictator" maximizes influence gain.

CASH FLOW
Allowance scales with Wealth stat. High wealth = more money each week. Buy Private Jet last — it's the biggest wealth + influence bomb in the game.

VIRALITY STRATEGY
High visibility + high controversy = viral. But type matters: beauty/elegance players go admired-viral, scandal players go hate-viral. Mysterious viral needs cult suspicion + obsession. Time posts in Year 3-4 for max impact — viral momentum carries between posts.

OBSESSION MANAGEMENT
Obsessors appear silently. Check the Obsessed window weekly. Admirers send cash (keep them at low level). Stalkers and exes become dangerous past 70. Weaponizing obsessors against rivals costs purity but destroys threats instantly.

PERSONALITY DRIFT
Narcissism above 60 + cult followers = God Complex archetype (uncontrollable cult growth). Empathy above 70 + ambition = Empath Weapon (convert rivals to allies). Watch Emotional Stability — below 25 triggers random scandal outbursts weekly.

MARRIAGE ROUTE
For Trophy Wife route: max beauty+elegance+wealth, then marry Edward or Marco. For First Lady: marry Sebastian (politician) or any candidate while dating William/Henri. Negotiate affairs clause early if you plan to keep boyfriends post-marriage.
